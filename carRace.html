Formula1 Car Race

The work required for this project the realization of a pilot of a formula 1 evolving on a virtual path. The race itself is regulated by software, the race manager. our pilot is written in C language. It will communicate with the GDC via its standard inputs and output, in text mode. The pilote uses AI (A star) algorithms to find the nearest path to the finishing line.
