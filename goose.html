Goose Game

This is a multiplayer board game where each player plays a student during his studies at school. The game is akin to the goose game based on the completion of a course made up of a succession of tiles. The students move with a dice roll which indicate the number of squares to move toward the finish. The goal of the players is, starting from the first square that represents admission to school, to succeed in their studies by arriving at the final graduation tile. The contents of the tiles influence the course with fast-forward bonus tiles or rollback penalty tiles stopping the game.
